What is coaching?

Brief information about coaching.

Coaching is one of the most powerful ways of communicating. It is one of the strongest tools to help people achieve their goals and make positive changes in their life and careers.

The strongest tool Coaches usually use is asking the right and powerful questions. It is a means for enabling the Client (the person who receives the Coaching, also sometimes called the “Coachee”) to think differently, raise awareness and look at the issues/problems from a different angle. Most important skills Coaches usually need to have is, to listen to the Client carefully and without any judgment by being neutral. When someone asks us for help the first thing usually coming to our mind is giving advice. It may be sometimes helpful. But this is not coaching. Coaches do not give advice. By asking powerful questions and using the right tools, Coaches raise awareness and responsibility in Clients and enable them to find the best answers/solutions themselves. It is a catalyst for positive change and I would say everyone needs a Coach.
